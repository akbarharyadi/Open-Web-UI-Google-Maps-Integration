# Google Maps Tool - Test Prompts

## Basic Place Search

1. "Find pizza restaurants in Brooklyn, New York"
2. "Where can I get coffee near Times Square?"
3. "Show me gas stations within 5km of Central Park"
4. "What are the best sushi restaurants in San Francisco?"
5. "Find hospitals in downtown Los Angeles"

## Place Search with Context

1. "I'm at JFK Airport and need to find a pharmacy nearby"
2. "Looking for vegetarian restaurants in Manhattan with good ratings"
3. "Find pet-friendly hotels in Seattle"
4. "Show me Italian restaurants near the Eiffel Tower"
5. "Where can I find a 24-hour convenience store in Las Vegas?"

## Directions

1. "How do I get from JFK Airport to Times Square?"
2. "Give me walking directions from Central Park to the Museum of Modern Art"
3. "Show me the driving route from Brooklyn to Manhattan"
4. "What's the best way to bike from Golden Gate Park to Fisherman's Wharf?"
5. "Transit directions from Penn Station to Yankee Stadium"

## Geocoding

1. "What are the coordinates of the Statue of Liberty?"
2. "Give me the latitude and longitude of the White House"
3. "Where is 1600 Amphitheatre Parkway, Mountain View?"
4. "Coordinates for the Golden Gate Bridge"
5. "What's the exact location of the Empire State Building?"

## Complex Multi-Step Queries

1. "Find the best rated steakhouse in Chicago and show me how to get there from O'Hare Airport"
2. "I want to visit the Louvre Museum in Paris. Give me its exact location and walking directions from the Eiffel Tower"
3. "Find a highly rated coffee shop near Stanford University and tell me the address and coordinates"
4. "What's the closest emergency room to Times Square and how do I get there from Grand Central Station?"
5. "Find Mexican restaurants in Austin, Texas, pick the highest rated one, and give me its details plus directions from Austin Airport"

## Edge Cases

1. "Find restaurants in XYZ123INVALID" (invalid location)
2. "How do I teleport from Earth to Mars?" (invalid travel mode)
3. "Find asdfqwerzxcv" (nonsense query)
4. "Directions from here to there" (vague locations)
5. "" (empty query)

## Natural Language Variations

1. "I'm hungry, where's the nearest burger joint?"
2. "Need to find a place to sleep tonight in Boston"
3. "What museums are open right now in DC?"
4. "Take me to the airport from my hotel in downtown"
5. "Where's the closest Starbucks?"

4. "How do I get to the Great Wall of China from Beijing?"
